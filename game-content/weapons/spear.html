General

The Spear is the second available weapon in the game, unlocked at level 4.

It deals low damage but attacks quickly, dealing bonus damage to fast enemies and slowing them down.
Its active ability makes the king attack extremely fast and regenerates his health. If the kings health is low, the duration of the ability doubles.
